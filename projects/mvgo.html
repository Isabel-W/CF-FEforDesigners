MVGO

About the project

The MVGO app is a native application for convenient public transport in Munich, offering real-time schedule information, route planning, and ticket purchasing. It simplifies travel by suggesting optimal routes and integrating additional mobility services like bike-sharing. This app is designed to enhance your transit experience by making navigation quick and straightforward.
Through the project, I had the opportunity to work in a design team, and as a result, I learned not only how to present my concepts and ideas to developers and stakeholders, but also how to challenge them with colleagues. This experience allowed me to further structure my approach, delve deeper into platform standards, and establish digital accessibility more comprehensively in my designs.
Duration: As of 06/2025
Project Setup: Agile product team with four focus areas
My Role: As part of the Design team I was responsible for connection information and integration of sharing providers.
